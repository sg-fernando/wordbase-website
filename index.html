Word Base

Play smart.

Step up to the plate and swing for the fences!
Designed for young spellers of all ages, this educational app combines the thrill of America's favorite pastime with interactive lessons to help kids improve their spelling skills. So whether they're an all-star or just starting out, get ready to hit a home run with Word Base!

Have fun!

With engaging animations, fun challenges, and plenty of opportunities to practice, kids will feel like they're playing a game while learning essential spelling skills.

Learn at your own pace.

With a variety of difficulty levels, kids can choose the right challenge for them. Whether they're just starting out or are ready to take on more advanced words, Word Base has something for everyone.

Track your progress.

With a built-in progress tracker, kids can see how they're doing and keep track of their accomplishments. They can even earn badges for completing challenges and reaching new milestones.

Customize your experience.

With the ability to create and add your own custom word lists, kids can practice specific words that are relevant to their interests or curriculum. Whether it's for school, a special project, or just for fun, the app's customization options allow kids to personalize their spelling experience.
